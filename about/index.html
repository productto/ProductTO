About ProductTO

ProductTO was founded in 2013 by Henry Dillon. ProductTO's purpose is to grow Toronto's local community of product people. It was one of the first product meetups in Toronto and grew into this monthly format.

Product managers so rarely get to talk about our work outside the context of our explicit product. This forum provides an opportunity to tackle our unique challenges from the context of different products, markets, and organizations.

The group is currently organized by May Wong.

Thank you to Intuit Canada for being our amazing ongoing host for this event!
